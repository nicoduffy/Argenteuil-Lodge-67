Welcome to Argenteuil Lodge

Established in 1880, Argenteuil Lodge No. 67 has been a cornerstone of brotherly love, relief, and truth in our community for over 144 years. We continue the ancient traditions of Freemasonry while actively participating in modern charitable works and community service.

Our Core Values

Brotherhood

Fostering lifelong bonds of friendship and mutual support among men of good character

Charity

Supporting our local community through charitable works and benevolent activities

Self-Improvement

Encouraging moral and intellectual growth through our time-honored teachings

Our Proud History

Argenteuil Lodge was chartered on November 17, 1880 under the jurisdiction of the Grand Lodge of Quebec. Over our 144-year history, we have:

  • Initiated countless Master Masons
  • Constaty raising funds for local charities
  • Maintained continuous operation since 144 years

Membership Information

We welcome men of good character who:

  • Believe in a Supreme Being
  • Are at least 18 years of age
  • Desire to improve themselves and their community

For inquiries about membership, please contact our Lodge Secretary at argenteuillodge@gmail.com .
